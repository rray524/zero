Recruitment Website Design

Recruitment Website Design is a very specialist area. A number of companies offer this service, but very few have 15 years experience in recruitment website solutions and management. As competition increases throughout the recruitment industry, it is essential that your recruitment website design not only reflects the standing of your business, but performs well and delivers the right candidate to fill your current roles. If your website is designed poorly and offers little functionality, then you will lose out.

It is also very important that your recruitment business embraces new technology and social media marketing, through the use of Twitter, Facebook, Blogging, Online PR and Mobile Compatible Recruitment Websites.

Our recruitment website systems deliver a professional design, powerful functionality, automatic social media integration and over 15 years experience in launching new recruitment businesses and improving existing recruitment companies online marketing strategy.

A recruitment agency website, to us, is a website that has many useful features intended to enhance your candidate's user experience when visiting your website and also includes other features targeted towards search engine marketing & optimisation. It also has to have a cutting edge, and professional design as well as being easy to navigate.

Some of the features included within our recruitment agency website system is an Online CV Database, Jobs By Email System, Contact Management System, Content Management System, Application Tracking, Automatic Job Posting to Third Party Systems and many more features too numerous to display here.

Search engine optimisation is also becoming more and more important for recruitment agency websites. We provide an ongoing search engine optimisation and submission service.

Performance v Looks

Whether you are a new start up recruitment business or an existing recruitment agency, you not only want a great looking website, but you also want a website that performs and gets you those top candidates and makes your placement fees. You also want to ensure that your social media marketing is linked to a mobile strategy and that your branding looks great on paper, but also as a Twitter icon! Make sure your website is set up for good search engine listings. There's a lot to think about, but our job is to make it sound as simple as we can!
