Nuclear Careers Online

Nuclear Careers Online is the first UK based jobs board for the Nuclear Industry.

This www.nuclearcareersonline.com is the full version of our Virtual Consultant Jobs Board System. It includes a full content management system, RSS Feeds, Free Jobs Feeds, Updateable Meta Tags per page, fixed URLs and a competition system that automatically records registrations and randomly selects a winner, based on certain, pre-set criteria.

The website was pre-launched during October 2008, with a full launch due in November 2008.
