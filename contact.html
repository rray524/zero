Getting to Us

If you want to visit us, then we're based on Lichfield Street, in a small building called The Cottage, which used to be Tamworth's old Fire Station, which adjoins a building known locally as "The White House".

We are on the way into Tamworth town centre and you can use the map to find us and if you are travelling from the South, North or the East, then head towards Junction 10 of the M42 and then take the A5 towards the town centre. There is parking at the rear of our offices if you are driving, but check with us first as it can get busy at times. There is also parking nearby in the town centre.

The train station is about a 10 minute walk away, although it may be best to jump in a Taxi outside the station and it only takes a few minutes by Taxi. Our opening hours are 0830-17.00 Mon-Thurs and 0830-1600 on Friday.

For technical issues outside office hours, contact support@zod.uk.com
